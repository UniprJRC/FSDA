HUwei

HUwei Computes weight function psi(u)/u for Huber

Description

More About

Function HUwei transforms vector u as follows \[ HUwei(u)= \left\{ \begin{array}{cc} 1 & |u/c| \leq 1 \\ c \times \frac{\mbox{sign}(u)}{u} & |u/c|>1 \\ \end{array} \right. \] See equation (2.32) p. 28 of Maronna et al. (2006)

References

Maronna, R.A., Martin D. and Yohai V.J. (2006), Robust Statistics, Theory and Methods, Wiley, New York.
