RKpsix

RKpsix Computes psi function times x for Rocke (translated Tukey's) biweight

Description

More About

function RKpsix transforms vector u as follows \[ RKpsix(u)= \left\{ \begin{array}{cc} u^2 & 0\leq |u| \leq M \\ u\left(1-\left( \frac{u-M}{c} \right)^2 \right)^2 & M \leq u \leq M+c \\ 0 & u > M+c \\ \end{array} \right. \]

References
