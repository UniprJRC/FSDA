RKpsi

Computes psi function for Rocke (translated Tukey's) biweight

More About

function RKpsi transforms vector u as follows \[ RKpsi(u)= \left\{ \begin{array}{cc} u & 0 \leq u \leq M \\ u \left(1-\left( \frac{u-M}{c} \right)^2 \right)^2 & M < u \leq M+c \\ 0 & u > M+c \\ \end{array} \right. \] See equation (2.19) p. 1332 of Rocke (1996).
