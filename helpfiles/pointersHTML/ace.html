ace

Computes alternative conditional expectation

Description

This function uses the alternating conditional expectations algorithm to find the transformations of y and X that maximise the proportion of variation in y explained by X. When X is a matrix, it is transformed so that its columns are equally weighted when predicting y.

References

Breiman, L. and Friedman, J.H. (1985), Estimating optimal transformations for multiple regression and correlation, "Journal of the American Statistical Association", Vol. 80, pp. 580-597.
